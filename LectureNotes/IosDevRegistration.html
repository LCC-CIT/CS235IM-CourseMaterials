Registering as an Apple iOS Developer

Apple iOS Developer University Program

  1. Your instructor adds you to the team of Apple developers at your college or university
  2. You will get an e-mail inviting you to join the team:
  3. In the e-mail there will be a link you need to click:
  4. You will be taken to the Apple Developer web site and will need to log in:


  5. If you don't have an apple ID, you will need to click on the "Create Apple ID" button, otherwise enter your Apple ID and password and click "Sign In".
  6. a message will pop up asking you to accept the invitation:

  7. Once you accept you will be taken to the "Lane Community College (Computer Information Technology) iOS Developer University Program" page.
    Click on "People" and you should see yourself listed as a member:

That's it!
