Registering an iOS Device for use with Xcode

If you want to test your iOS app on a device, regardless of whether you are developing using Visual Studio with the Xamarin platform or using XCode,  you need to register the device on the Apple Developer site.

  1. Create a certificate
    1. Go to the Apple Developer web site and log in.
    2. On the left side, click on "Certificates, IDs & Profiles"
    3. On the left side, under the heading "Devices", select the type of device you want to add
    4. Next you need to get the UUID of your device.
      1. Connect your device to your computer using a USB cable
      2. Open Xcode, click on "Window", then select "Devices and Simulators"


      3. Select your device from the list on the left, then highlight the Identifier (UUID) and press cmd-C to copy it.


    5. Back on the Apple Developer web page, enter the device name and UUID, then click "Continue"

You're done! You can use this device with Xcode or Visual Studio.
